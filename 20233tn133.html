Bryan Konietzko y Michael Dante DiMartino

Avatar: The Last Airbender is an American animated television series produced by Nickelodeon Animation Studio. It aired for three seasons on Nickelodeon, from February 2005 to July 2008.

The series follows the adventures of Aang, the successor to a long line of Avatars, and his friends in their quest to save the world from the ruthless Fire Nation.
